Chapter 1: Introduction

I am a big fan of Simon Tatham's Portable Puzzle Collection, and thought it would be a great to have it working on my mobile phone. Since there was no port yet, I went ahead and wrote one myself. It took me more than one year of planning and a few months of coding but I was able to do it.

I only wrote the Symbian S60 port, not the puzzles. For problems regarding the puzzles, please contact the original author.
